About Say Say Photography

Hey There! Pleased to meet you. Here is a little about me.

Shaw Photography is about the pursuit of perfection when it comes to Architectural Photography. Using creativity, light and composition to represent form and space as it was intended by its creator. ​ There are so many variables when it comes to capturing a building or space that can affect the way it looks. Things like the equipment and type of lens used, the angle, the time of day or even the time of year, the weather, changes in the surrounding environment and a host of other factors. Typically my clients are seeking images to apply for awards, or to use in a variety of promotional ways such as billboards, magazines and web and, as such, want a photographer who understands the complexities of what goes into Architecture Photography, and has the skills and experience to represent their work as they intended it. ​ My clients love the work they do. In fact it is not just their work, it is their passion. There are literally weeks, months, and years of work that go into their projects. Even beyond that, my images will go into representing my clients' brand that they have spent their career building. Is this you? Are you passionate about the work you do? If so, then capturing your passion is my passion!
